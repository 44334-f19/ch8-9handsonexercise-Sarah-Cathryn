Description Set in the late 1800s-early 1900s, The Secret of Moonacre is the story of one Maria Merryweather. When her father dies, Maria and her nurse Miss Heliotrope are sent the the countryside to live with her uncle, Sir Benjamin Merryweather. Upon arrival she is attacked in her carriage by the ruffian scum of an opposing family, the de Noir clan. It is up to her to unite the families, break a curse and bring back magic to the Valley of Moonacre. Can she do it?
